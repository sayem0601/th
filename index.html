History of Titumir House

Mir Nisar Ali Titumir, a Bengali Muslim leader and revolutionary, is celebrated for his courageous resistance against British colonial rule in the 19th century. Revered as a national hero in Bangladesh, his unwavering commitment to independence has left an indelible mark on the nation's history. Titumir House at Rangpur Cadet College is named in honor of this legendary freedom fighter, ensuring his legacy continues to inspire future generations. The house stands as a beacon of excellence, with a storied history of both academic and extracurricular success. Motto and Emblem

The motto of Titumir House at Rangpur Cadet College, "We Are Fearless," resonates with the heart and soul of its cadets. The emblem, a powerful clenched fist, symbolizes strength, unity, and unwavering determination. It embodies the indomitable spirit of the cadets, who stand fearless in the face of challenges, ready to conquer any obstacle that comes their way. The phrase "We Are Fearless" captures the essence of the courage and bravery instilled in each cadet, shaping them into future leaders and steadfast defenders of their nation.
